Our Mission

    National journal of Applied Physics is dedicated to publishing the latest research and innovations in applied physics. Our journal provides a platform for researchers, scientists, and academicians to share breakthrough discoveries and cutting-edge technologies across a wide range of applied physics disciplines.

    We cover topics such as quantum mechanics, semiconductor devices, photonics, nanotechnology, and materials science, offering high-impact papers that advance the field. Our goal is to drive scientific progress through rigorous peer-reviewed articles, special issues, and review papers that highlight significant developments in applied physics.

    Join us in exploring the frontiers of applied physics, where theory meets experimentation, and new applications in science and technology are born.
