Submission Guidelines

General Information

"National journal of Applied Physics welcomes the submission of original research articles, review articles, and technical notes in all areas of applied physics. We aim to publish high-quality research that advances the field and contributes to scientific knowledge. All submitted manuscripts undergo a rigorous peer review process.

Submission Process

  1. Manuscript Preparation:
    • Manuscripts should be written in English and formatted according to the journal's style guide.
    • The text must be double-spaced, using a standard font (e.g., Times New Roman, 12-point size).
    • Use a standard referencing style (e.g., APA, IEEE) consistently throughout the manuscript.
    • Figures and tables should be included in the main document, each with a descriptive caption.
  2. Cover Letter:
    • Include a cover letter with the submission, summarizing the main findings and significance of the work.
    • State that the work is original and has not been published elsewhere.
  3. Author Information:
    • Provide full names, affiliations, and contact information for all authors.
    • Indicate the corresponding author who will handle correspondence regarding the manuscript.
  4. Supplementary Materials:
    • If applicable, include supplementary materials (e.g., datasets, additional figures) to support the manuscript.

Submission Method

All manuscripts must be submitted electronically via our online submission system. Follow the instructions on the website to create an account and submit your manuscript.

Peer Review Process

Upon submission, the manuscript will undergo an initial review for suitability. If deemed suitable, it will be sent for peer review by experts in the field. Authors may be asked to revise their manuscript based on reviewer feedback.

Ethical Considerations

Authors must ensure that their work complies with ethical standards. Plagiarism, data fabrication, and any form of misconduct are strictly prohibited. Any conflicts of interest must be disclosed in the cover letter.
